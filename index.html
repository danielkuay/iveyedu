Our Philosophy

A specialist preschool dedicated
entirely to early childhood

🌱Reggio
+ EYFS

Unlike larger international schools that span from preschool to secondary, IVEY is a dedicated specialist preschool. Every decision we make — from our classroom design to our daily meals — is made with one purpose: giving children aged 2–6 the very best possible start in Kuala Lumpur.

Our Reggio-Inspired + Inquiry approach is built on the belief that children are naturally competent learners. We invite every child to explore, examine, question and investigate — in beautiful, age-appropriate indoor and outdoor spaces carefully prepared to spark curiosity and independent thinking.

Our dedicated and highly skilled early years educators work together to give each child the best possible foundation for all future learning — not just academically, but socially, emotionally, creatively and physically.

🧠

Communication & Language

Developing every child's listening, understanding and speaking skills through rich conversation, storytelling, and meaningful play — the foundation for all future learning.

💛

Personal, Social & Emotional

Collaborative group projects and purposeful play build empathy, resilience, self-confidence, and the social skills children need to thrive in diverse, multicultural environments.

🎭

Expressive Arts & Design

Through Reggio's "Hundred Languages of Children", we nurture creativity via art, music, dramatic play, painting, sculpting and crafting — honouring every child's unique voice.

🏃

Physical Development

Spacious indoor and outdoor environments with open-ended, organic materials build strength, coordination and confidence through active, exploratory play.

Our Curriculum

The EYFS framework — internationally
recognised early years excellence

IVEY follows the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework for England — the same gold-standard curriculum used by the top international schools in Kuala Lumpur — enriched with the Reggio Emilia philosophy for a truly holistic, child-led learning experience.

The EYFS framework guides children's learning across seven key areas of development. At IVEY, we nurture all seven areas every day through carefully planned activities, rich environments, and inspiring educators.

🗣️

Communication & Language

Listening, attention, understanding and speaking — developed through rich conversations, stories, songs and playful interaction every day.

💪

Physical Development

Building strength, coordination and confidence through active play, both large motor outdoors and fine motor craft activities indoors.

🤝

Personal, Social & Emotional

Self-regulation, building relationships, and managing feelings — critical for lifelong happiness, confidence and success in a multicultural world.

📖

Literacy

A language-rich environment prepares every child to read, write and communicate fluently, with a genuine love of books and storytelling.

🔢

Mathematics

Number, shape, space and measure are explored through hands-on, sensory activities — making maths a joyful, concrete discovery.

🌏

Understanding the World

Children explore people, communities, technology and the natural world — developing cultural curiosity, scientific thinking and global awareness.

🎨

Expressive Arts & Design

Creativity through art, music, movement, drama and role play — honouring every child's unique way of communicating and experiencing the world.

🌿

Reggio Emilia Extension

Our Reggio-Inspired approach adds project-based inquiry, the "environment as a third teacher", and collaborative learning beyond the standard EYFS framework.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Can't find your answer here? Call us on 03-2780 6872 or book a tour and ask us in person.

What ages does IVEY Preschool accept?

IVEY accepts children from 2 to 6 years old across four tailored programmes: Pre-Nursery (2–3), Nursery (3–4), Pre-Kinder (4–5) and Kinder (5–6). Each programme is designed to match your child's natural stage of development.

What curriculum does IVEY follow?

We follow the UK Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework enriched by the Reggio Emilia philosophy. We also offer Mandarin and Bahasa Malaysia immersion classes, preparing children for Malaysia's multicultural landscape.

Where is IVEY located?

IVEY has two branches in Kuala Lumpur: our flagship at Plaza Arkadia, Desa Parkcity (G-G-08, 52200 KL) and our new branch at Jalan Pantai Prima, Pantai Dalam, 59200 KL. Both locations are easily accessible and in vibrant, family-friendly communities.

How do I book a school tour?

Simply complete the enquiry form on this page or call us at 03-2780 6872 / 011-1633 6617. Our admissions team will contact you within one business day to arrange a private, personalised school tour at your preferred time.

What are IVEY's session times and hours?

We offer three flexible session options: Half Day (8:30am–12:30pm), Extended Hours (8:30am–3:00pm), and Full Day (8:30am–6:00pm), Monday to Friday. Our full day option includes all meals prepared by our in-house chef.

Are meals provided at IVEY?

Yes! All full-day and extended-hours students receive wholesome, nutritious meals prepared daily by our dedicated in-house chef. We believe a healthy, well-fed child is a happy, focused learner.

What makes IVEY different from larger international schools?

IVEY is a dedicated specialist preschool — not a section of a larger K–12 school. This means every resource, every educator and every decision is focused entirely on early childhood. We offer a more intimate, child-centred environment with a low teacher-to-child ratio and personalised care.

Is IVEY suitable for expat families new to KL?

Absolutely. IVEY has a warmly multicultural and international community. Many of our families are expats from across Asia, Europe and beyond. Our diverse, English-medium environment with Mandarin and Bahasa Malaysia immersion makes it an ideal first school for children new to Malaysia.
