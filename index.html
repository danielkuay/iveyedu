Welcome to IVEY

More than just a daycare
or a traditional preschool

🌱Reggio
+ EYFS

At IVEY, we strive to provide an excellent foundation for our students, where learning is a self-initiated move for the child, anytime and anywhere. The potential within each child is immeasurable — it is crucial that such potential is unmasked progressively by developing the inquisitive mind into a young confident adult.

Our educational methodology — Reggio Inspired + Inquiry Approach — encourages children to be INQUISITIVE, INDEPENDENT and ENTHUSIASTIC in their course of learning. We lead children to learning through purposeful play and discovery, with every encounter carefully planned to fit into our rich, stimulating settings.

🧠

Cognitive

Our curriculum is ignited by children's ideas and theories. When surrounded with tools and materials, children venture into their own wonders and investigations — developing cognitive skills innately and naturally.

💛

Social & Emotional

Learning at IVEY is woven into the social context and enhanced by collaborative group projects. Caring educators and a family-oriented ambience is essential for the social and emotional comfort of every child.

🎭

Creative

Creativity is encouraged through Reggio's philosophy of "The Hundred Language of Children" — art, dramatic play, painting, music, construction, inventive writing and crafting.

🏃

Physical

Our spaces are equipped with open-ended materials and raw textures. Large body and small motor opportunities meet every child's wide range of abilities and interests.

Our Curriculum

EYFS (UK) + Reggio Emilia —
a dynamic world-class foundation

IVEY's curriculum is built on the Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) UK framework — enriched by the Reggio Emilia philosophy for a holistic, child-led experience covering all seven EYFS areas of learning.

🗣️

Communication & Language

A language-rich environment developing listening, attention, understanding and speaking through conversations, stories and songs every day.

💪

Physical Development

Great outdoor and spacious indoor learning spaces develop strength, coordination and confidence through active, exploratory play.

🤝

Personal, Social & Emotional

Self-regulation and relationships — critical for lifelong confidence and success in Malaysia's multicultural world.

📖

Literacy

Building a genuine love of books and reading through a language-rich, story-filled environment that prepares every child for primary school.

🔢

Mathematics

Number, shape, space and measure explored through hands-on, sensory activities — making maths a joyful, concrete discovery.

🌏

Understanding the World

Children explore people, communities, technology and nature — developing curiosity, scientific thinking and global awareness.

🎨

Expressive Arts & Design

Through Reggio's "Hundred Languages of Children" — art, music, movement, drama and role play honour every child's unique voice.

🌿

Reggio Emilia Extension

Project-based inquiry, "environment as third teacher", and collaborative investigations that go beyond the standard EYFS framework.

FAQs

Everything you need to know

Still have questions? Call us at 03-2780 6872 or come visit us for a school tour.

What ages does IVEY accept?

IVEY accepts children from 2 to 6 years old across four programmes: Pre-Nursery (2–3), Nursery (3–4), Pre-Kinder (4–5) and Kinder (5–6) — each designed to match your child's stage of development.

What curriculum does IVEY follow?

We follow the UK Early Years Foundation Stage (EYFS) framework enriched by the Reggio Emilia philosophy, plus Mandarin and Bahasa Malaysia immersion classes.

Where are IVEY's branches in KL?

Our flagship is at G-G-08, Plaza Arkadia, Desa Parkcity (52200 KL). Our new Bangsar South branch is at Jalan Pantai Prima, Bangsar South (59200 KL).

How do I book a school tour?

Complete the enquiry form below or call 03-2780 6872 / 011-1633 6617. Our admissions team will contact you within one business day to arrange a private tour.

What session times are available?

Half Day (8:30am–12:30pm), Extended Hours (8:30am–3:00pm), and Full Day (8:30am–6:00pm), Monday to Friday. Full day includes wholesome meals from our in-house chef.

Are meals provided at IVEY?

Yes! Full-day and extended-hours students receive wholesome, nutritious meals prepared daily by our dedicated in-house chef. A healthy, well-fed child is a happy, focused learner.

Is IVEY suitable for expat families?

Absolutely. IVEY has a warmly multicultural and international community with families from across Asia, Europe and beyond. Our English-medium environment with Mandarin and Bahasa Malaysia immersion is ideal for children new to Malaysia.

What makes IVEY different?

IVEY is a dedicated specialist preschool — not a section of a larger K–12 school. Every resource and educator is focused entirely on early childhood (ages 2–6), creating a more intimate, personalised and nurturing start to life.
