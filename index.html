Our Philosophy

Learning through wonder, play & real discovery

🌱Reggio
Inspired

Our Reggio-Inspired + Inquiry approach views every child as a naturally competent learner, eager to shape their own understanding of the world.

We build experiences around each child's curiosity — combining social interaction, project-based investigations, and purposeful play.

🧠

Cognitive Development

Children explore and question the world, developing critical thinking through self-directed investigation.

💛

Social & Emotional Growth

Collaborative projects and group play build empathy, resilience, and lifelong social confidence.

🎭

Creative Expression

Art, music, dramatic play and crafting let children speak in a hundred different languages.

🏃

Physical Exploration

Spacious indoor and outdoor spaces support gross motor play, fine motor skills, and sensory discovery.

Why Choose IVEY

The uniqueness of our school

📐

Diverse & Innovative Curriculum

A well-balanced curriculum built on the UK EYFS framework, fused with the world-renowned Reggio Emilia philosophy for a genuinely global education.

👩‍🏫

Qualified & Caring Educators

Head teachers hold recognised early childhood qualifications. Our low student-to-teacher ratio ensures every child receives individual attention and love.

🏡

Stimulating Environments

A unique community village layout fosters mixed-age interaction. Provocation settings spark self-motivated discovery over traditional instruction.

🥗

Nutritious In-House Meals

Our in-house chef prepares fresh, wholesome meals daily — giving children the energy to explore, play, and learn at their very best.

🌿

Indoor & Outdoor Spaces

Spacious outdoor play areas and thoughtfully designed indoor environments filled with open-ended materials fuel limitless exploration.

🌐

Multilingual Immersion

Mandarin and Bahasa Malaysia immersion classes are woven into daily life, preparing children to thrive in Malaysia's multicultural landscape.
